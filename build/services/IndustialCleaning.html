INDUSTIAL CLEANING

STEVGNIA INTERCONTINENTAL VENTURES LTD is a leading provider of high-quality industrial cleaning services based in Lagos, Nigeria. Situated in the heart of Agbara Estate, the company offers tailored cleaning solutions for a wide range of industries, ensuring clean and safe environments for its clients. With a dedicated team of trained professionals, STEVGNIA specializes in maintaining industrial facilities, manufacturing plants, warehouses, and commercial spaces with precision and efficiency.

STEVGNIA INTERCONTINENTAL VENTURES LTD prioritizes safety, reliability, and environmental sustainability in all its services. The company is committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness while adhering to industry regulations. Through the use of advanced cleaning technologies and eco-friendly products, STEVGNIA ensures optimal results while minimizing its environmental footprint.
